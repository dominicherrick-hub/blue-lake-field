🛰 Channel 1 — KICK System + Direct Mail Your Unfair Advantage
Why this is different from any competitor No other pressure washer in Lake County is scanning GIS aerials to identify stained roofs, pulling property data, and sending a personalized postcard with the property's own photo. This is a "how did you know about my roof?" moment — and it converts at 3–5x the rate of generic mailers.

KICK Scan Workflow (Monthly)

  1. Open Lake County GIS Viewer
  2. Select target neighborhood. Activate 2025 Aerials layer.
  3. Use Identify tool — click each parcel with a noticeably dark roof
  4. Record: address, owner, AltKey, built year, market value, homestead status
  5. Confirm no re-roof permit in GovWell (Eustis) or Lake County permits (unincorporated)
  6. Score Level 1–4 based on roof age + visual finding
  7. Create CRM record using crm_template_v2.html
  8. Add lead to direct mail queue

Direct Mail Cadence — 3-Wave System

  • Wave 1 (Day 1): Roof Alert postcard. Property-specific opening: "We noticed your roof at [address]…" Include aerial finding, age, offer.
  • Wave 2 (Day 30): Social proof angle. "Your neighbors on [street] recently had their roofs cleaned…" Before/after photo from a nearby job if available.
  • Wave 3 (Day 60): Urgency + final offer. "We're finishing work in [neighborhood] this month — if we can add your home to our schedule, here's a discounted rate."
Most responses come after Wave 2 or 3. Don't stop at Wave 1. The 3-touch rule is real.

📍 Channel 2 — Google Business Profile Free Inbound Leads

Setup Priority (Week 1)

  1. Claim/verify GBP at business.google.com
  2. Category: "Pressure Washing Service" (primary)
  3. Service area: Eustis, Mount Dora, Tavares, Leesburg, Clermont, Apopka
  4. Write keyword-rich description (see GBP checklist file)
  5. Upload 10+ named before/after photos immediately
  6. Add all services with descriptions and price ranges
  7. Get review link → save as phone text template
  8. Target: 15 reviews in first 30 days via past customers

Ongoing GBP Actions (Weekly)

  • 📸 Post 1 before/after per week — name files with keywords before upload
  • ⭐ Text review request to every customer within 2 hours of job completion
  • 💬 Respond to every review — 5-star and negative
  • 📅 Check GBP Insights once/week — track Searches and Calls
  • 🙋 Answer any Q&A questions posted by users
At 15+ reviews you should appear in the Google 3-pack for "pressure washing Eustis FL." At 30+ reviews, you'll hold it. Most competitors in Eustis have under 10.
🏘 Channel 3 — Nextdoor & Referrals Neighborhood Trust

Nextdoor Strategy

  • Create personal profile + Nextdoor Business page
  • Post a before/after after every job in that neighborhood (with homeowner permission)
  • Respond to every "who does pressure washing?" thread — within the hour if possible
  • Offer a neighbor rate: "Since I'm already mobilized on [street], I can do nearby homes at a $50 discount this week."
  • Don't hard-sell. Be the helpful neighbor who happens to wash roofs.

Referral Program

  • Ask every customer immediately after the annual plan pitch — not at a random time
  • Reward structure: $50 off their next renewal + $50 off the new customer's first job
  • Script: "If any neighbors have been asking who did your roof, I'd love a referral. I'll give them $50 off and credit $50 to your next renewal."
  • Thank referrers immediately by text when the referral books (see follow-up templates)
  • Track referrals in the CRM referral section
🤝 Channel 4 — Strategic Partnerships Recurring Volume

Real Estate Agents (High Priority)

Pre-listing cleanups are urgent, high-margin, and repeat. One agent who lists 20 homes/year can send you 3–4 jobs/month.

  • Identify 10 active agents in Eustis/Lake County on Zillow/Realtor.com
  • Call or email each: "I specialize in pre-listing exterior cleaning in Lake County. I'd love to offer a free demo on one of your upcoming listings."
  • Build a "Pre-Listing Package" (house + driveway bundle, fast turnaround guarantee)
  • Ask to be their go-to referral — exclusive relationship if possible

Property Managers (Steady Income)

Rental properties need regular cleaning. Property managers just need reliable, insured, and on-time. No sales pitch required beyond showing up twice without problems.

  • Your KICK system already flags rental properties (owner address ≠ property address)
  • Contact owners of rental properties directly — they manage remotely and need a trusted local vendor
  • Search for property management companies in Eustis/Leesburg — introduce yourself by email or call
  • Offer a "rental maintenance program" — annual clean, photo report sent to owner

HOA Management Companies (Long-Term Play)

Getting on a preferred vendor list for one HOA-managed community can generate 10–20 referrals per year from a single relationship. You already have an opening — the HOA compliance angle you've used in Blue Lake Estates is exactly the right foot in the door. Introduce yourself to the HOA management company as the vendor who handles algae/mildew complaints proactively. Bring before/after photos from the neighborhood.
