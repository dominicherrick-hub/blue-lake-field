Upload 10+ before/after photos immediately

Respond to every review — 5-star and negative

Phase 5 — GBP Posts (Weekly Cadence)
GBP posts expire after 7 days. One post per week keeps your profile "active" — a ranking signal Google looks for.
Weekly
Post a before/after photo every week
Caption formula: "[Service] in [City] — [one sentence about what we found] → [one sentence about the result]. Call or book online for a free quote. 📞 (352) ___-____"

Example: "Roof soft wash in Eustis — this 20-year-old roof had significant algae buildup on the north face. After treatment, the original color is back. Free quotes for Lake County homeowners."
Post a seasonal offer or tip monthly
Florida's rainy season (June–Sept) is your best opening: "Lake County's wet season accelerates algae growth on roofs. We're booking August cleans now — schedule early." Post as "Offer" type in GBP for extra visibility.
Check GBP Insights weekly (5 min)
GBP → Insights shows: how many people searched for you, called, clicked directions. Track "Searches" and "Calls" week over week. This tells you if your optimization is working — expect to see it grow after Phase 1–3 are done.

One-Time Setup

Create Facebook Business Page

Add to Nextdoor Business (free)
business.nextdoor.com — create a free business page for your service area. This puts you in the neighborhood feed. Separate from your personal Nextdoor profile (keep both).
Submit to HomeAdvisor / Angi (free tier only)
Create a free profile — do NOT pay for leads yet. Their free listings rank in Google and provide a citation for your NAP. Paid lead programs on Angi are overpriced for solo operators — skip those.
